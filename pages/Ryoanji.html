Rock Garden

A fence surrounds three sides of an oblong garden of about 1,000 square meters, with white sand spread over the ground. The simple arrangement of just 15 stones and white sand expresses the world of Zen. The fifteen rocks are arranged so there is always one rock that cannot be seen regardless of the angle.

Simple yet Powerful

With its low white earthen wall in the background, it is said that this garden expresses islands amidst the expansive ocean. Although limited in space, it gives visitors a sense of infinity.
